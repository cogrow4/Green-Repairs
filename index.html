ABOUT US

Your Trusted Tech Repair Partner

Founded in 2022 in the heart of Gordon, Tasmania, Green Repairs has been providing top-notch tech repair services to our local community. What started as a small repair shop has grown into a trusted name in device repairs across the region.

Our mission is simple: to provide high-quality, affordable repairs while reducing electronic waste. We believe in fixing, not replacing, to save you money and help the environment.
